1.when the contents of the div is more than the div then the contents will overflow from the div

2.if u want that the contents adjust themselves according to the space available in the div then u can use the property the property of overflow wrap
overflow-wrap: break-word;
we can also use hidden ,scroll etc.

3.in case words are overflowing we can use 
overflow-wrap: break-word;

4.overflow wrap can take two values 
a.normal...let the words flow
b.word break
