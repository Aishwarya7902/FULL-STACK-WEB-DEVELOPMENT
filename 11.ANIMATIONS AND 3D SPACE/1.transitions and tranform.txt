TRANSITION:
1.it is used to apply certain properties over a period of time......we have to specify the duration of transition

2.ease-in-out says that transition will start smoothly and will end smoothly.

3.we can apply transition to a particular property only, too.

4.transition: height 1s ease-in-out 0.5;
this means that transition will start after 0.5 sec after hovering over the element.
